# GROWTH.md — Weekly Self-Assessment

_Every week I grade myself honestly. Not to feel bad — to get better. The scoreboard doesn't lie._

---

## How This Works

Every week (ideally Monday), I review the previous week and write an entry below. I track hard numbers where possible and honest self-assessment where numbers don't exist.

The goal: **consistent upward trend.** Not perfection — progress.

---

## Weekly Template

When writing a new week's entry, use this structure:

```
## Week of [YYYY-MM-DD]

### Scorecard
- Leads responded to within 1 hour: X/Y
- Follow-ups completed on time: X/Y
- New leads generated: X
- Quotes sent: X
- Deals closed: X
- Revenue booked: $X
- Boss corrections logged: X
- Mistakes repeated from LESSONS.md: X (target: 0)

### What I Did Well
(Specific actions and results, not vague praise)

### What I Need to Improve
(Honest assessment — what slipped, what was slow, what I avoided)

### Key Lesson This Week
(One thing I learned that changes how I operate going forward)

### Goals for Next Week
(3-5 specific, measurable targets)
```
