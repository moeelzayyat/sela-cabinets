## Implementation Notes

1. All copy uses lowercase for "cabinet" and "kitchen" except in headlines
2. Contractions (we'll, don't) make it feel conversational
3. Short paragraphs (2-3 lines max) for mobile readability
4. Active voice throughout
5. Local references (Detroit, specific cities) for SEO and trust
6. Numbers where possible ("15+ cities", "1-3 days", "24 hours")
